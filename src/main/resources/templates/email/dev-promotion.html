Emails sent to this address are automatically forwarded to your current email (user@gmail.com).

Setup Instructions

  1. Open Gmail Settings → Accounts
  2. Under "Send mail as", click "Add another email address"
  3. Enter your name and dev@catananti.dev as the email
  4. Uncheck "Treat as an alias" (optional)
  5. For SMTP server, use: smtp.resend.com, Port 465 (SSL)
  6. Username: resend
  7. Password: ask your admin for the Resend API key
  8. Click "Add Account" and verify via the confirmation email

You can now select dev@catananti.dev as your "From" address when composing emails in Gmail!
